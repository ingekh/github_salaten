White Bellbird

Don't let the its appearance fool you. It is literally the loudest bird known to man, and it sounds like something between a smoke detector and factory alarm.

A very known animal native to Africa. Even though it's a known one, it's still weird looking. Its long neck helps it to eat from tall trees.

Capybara

The worlds biggest rodent. It doesn't care, and it doesn't mind anyone until provoked.
